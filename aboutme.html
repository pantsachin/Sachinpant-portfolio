About Me

After completing Mechanical Engineering from Delhi College of Engineering in 2019 I started working in an operations firm. And 6 months down the line I didn't enjoy that work much and I left that job in pursuit of something else. Then came the coronavirus and changed the whole world.

It was during this time when I decided that why not explore something which I thought I could never do. It was Coding, and I came across Neog.Camp. Now I am learning Full Stack Web Dev with neog.camp. Till now the journey has been fantastic.

My College Journey

In the four years of Engineering I was an active figure in the sporting community of my university. Being the Treasurer of Sports Council of DTU. I was bestowed upon with a lot of responsibilities involving high monetary exchanges whether it be in the form of development of sporting facilities in the University premises or to conudct all India level sports fests.

I published a research paper on the Tribological Analysis of a Cricket Ball with respect to a Cricket Pitch in my final year. It was also my final year major project done under the guidance of my mentor Dr. R.C. Singh.

Apart from participating in various college events I played prestigious club cricket in the DDCA league of Delhi and also represented our University in All India University (AIU) Sports tournament.

All in all, over the course of 4 years along with all my co-curricular activites I managed to secure an aggregate percentage of 75.7% .

My School Journey

All my school life I took part in one acitivity or some other. Staybacks was a common thing for me. From theatre to sports to quiz competitions. I was always up for anything.

I served in the executive committee of our senior school overtaking various responsibilities. In school, due to my burning passion for cricket I was given the sports portfolio.

Another activity which instilled confidence in me to face anybody was Theatre. I truly believe in Shakespeare's quote, "All the world's a stage!". I participated and won in many inter-school level street and stage plays.

I secured 94.8% in my CBSE 12th standard board exams
